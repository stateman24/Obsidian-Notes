# **Tacit Theories**

**Created: 2026-01-04:21:33**
**Tags:** #tacit-thoery #f-notes
____
Tacit thories are the informal and even unconsious way you understand or make sense of the world (i.e you world view theories) that are not expilcit, spoken or know to you without an intentional reflection. This include you working assumptions or conceptualization you have about things.
Everyone operates form a tacit theories about people and the world 
In term of reasearch tacit theories can containt your research in a variety of ways which can be a good or bad thing depending on you context
