# Writing is the only thing that matters

**Created: 2026-01-02: 21:51**
**Tags:** #note-taking #writing 
____
- Student view the need writing as a form of examination because by writing student demonstrate what they learn, think critically, and develop ideas 
- And in this mindset, writing a paper for research is a skill that needs to be acquired 
- All academic papers a based on this view. 
- Studying is an independent research instead of it preparing a student for an independent research 
- Studying, done properly, is research, because it is about gaining insight that cannot be anticipated and will be shared within the scientific community under public scrutiny.
- Writing is a medium of research i.e production and studying is the research
- When writing is all the matter it enables the person to be deliberate and have a clear purpose on the activity someone engages in
- If you change your mind about the importance of writing, you will also change your mind about everything else. Even if you decide never to write a single line of a manuscript, you will improve your reading, thinking and other intellectual skills just by doing everything as if nothing counts other than writing.
