# **Remember to Pray for Others**

**Created: 2026-01-04:10:46**
**Tags:** #watchtower-study
____
- Paul took his time to pray for others Eph. 1:16, He also prayed for individuals like Timothy 2 Tim. 1:3
- We might at times forget to pray for others because life in this system is hectic. We can get so distracted by our problems that we pray only about our own needs
- Praying for others has a powerful effect James 5:16
- There are several examples of this like Luke 22:32, Philemon 22
- Praying for others helps us develop “tender compassion.”
- Praying for others helps us to put our own problems in the proper perspective. Praying for others reminds us that others too are facing challenges (Philippians 2:3,4). That fact keeps us from becoming overly focused on our own problems
- We can include ppl with health challenges, our fellow believers who endure opposition also for ppl battling with old age 
- We can also pray for those taking the lead in Jehovah’s organization
- Making general prayer for our brothers even though we don’t them individually is also pleasing to Jehovah Eph. 6:18
- It is also important that we have a balance view of how we pray for others
