### The 5 Filter Framework 
- **Change Trajectory** - How does the goal change the course of my life?, Does it fundamentally change the my future path?
- **Create Leverage** - Will success here make everything else easier? 
- **Sacrifice** - Does it demand me to give something up?
- **Trigger Domino Effect** - Will other wins naturally follow?
- **Show Progress in 90 day** - Can I measure meaningful progress quickly? 

>  Dream needs structure. Strategy bridges intentions and outcome 

### Strategic Planning Model
- Vision - See the Destination 
- Focus - Eliminate distractions 
- Action - Execution relentlessly

- What becomes easier 
- What problem disappear 
- Who would I have become
