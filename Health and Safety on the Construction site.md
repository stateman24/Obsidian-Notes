# **Health and Safety Regulations on the Construction site**

**Tags**: #p-notes #health-and-safety #construction-mangement 
**Date Created: 2026-01-08:15:11**
____
Health and Safety Regulations is meant to provide construction site activities with a well-designed framework, rules and regulations to govern, monitor, and improve the quality, safety, and efficiency of construction projects. Through robust safety planning and comprehensive risk assessment, head-on safety regulations helps site managers to proactively prevent accidents or tragedies on site.
