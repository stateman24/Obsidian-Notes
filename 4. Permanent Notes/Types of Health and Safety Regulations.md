# **Types of Health and Safety Regulations**

**Tags**: #p-notes #construction-mangement #health-and-safety 
**Date Created: 2026-01-08:15:25**
____
There are several types of Health and Safety Regulations in the construction industry. They include:

 1. International Labour Standard
 2. Occupational Safety and Health Act
 3. Construction Regulation 2003
 4. Safety and Health Regulations of Construction
