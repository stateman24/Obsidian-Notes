# **How writing should be viewed as student**

**Tags**: #p-notes #writing 
**Date Created: 2026-01-18:13:10**
____
As student we should view writing as a medium of research in which studying is a research. It is important we make view writing as some thing that matters when we study since with will make the person to be deliberate and have a clear purpose on the study being carried out. Studying is a research because it is about gaining insight about a particular topic and writing is a medium with we study since it allows us to think about what we are studying and put them in perspective.  Writing will definitely improve our reading, thinking and intellectual skill when we prioritize writing out what we study as a student
