# **International standard**

**Tags**: #p-notes 
**Date Created: 2026-01-08:16:17**
____
International standards are standards that are developed by globally recognized bodies to provide universally acceptable benchmarks for quality and safety in engineering and construction. They are essential for projects that require multinational countries' cross-border engineering practices. Examples are:
- ISO(International Organization of Standardization) 
- BSI(British Standard Institution) 
- ICC(International Code Council) 
- ACI(American Concrete Institution) and so on.
