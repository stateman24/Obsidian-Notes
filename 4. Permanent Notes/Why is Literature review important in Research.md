# **Why is Literature review important in Research**

**Tags**: #p-notes #research #writing #lit-review
**Date Created: 2026-01-18:19:31**
____
What makes literature review important in scholarly research is that it helps researchers to 
1. **cite** relevant writings relating to the topic being researched on
2. **compare** and **contrast** existing arguments 
3. **critique** the strength and weakness of relevant scholarship to the topic at hand
4. and **connect** scholarships in a way that suites the new research.
It helps the researchers to demonstrate command of the field, clarify the position of the current work and justify the need for the new research.
