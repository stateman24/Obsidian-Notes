# **Method of managing health and safety and Project information**

**Tags**: #p-notes #construction-mangement #health-and-safety 
**Date Created: 2026-01-08:15:56**
____
For effective [[Information Regulations on Construction site]] there are several methods employed by site, engineers and professionals to effectively regulate information on site. Some of these include:
1. Stakeholders Relations: This involves engaging stakeholders from the onset to foster shared understanding and accountability. It means involving them in key matters such as: appointments disciplinary actions and so on. 
2. Site meeting: This is a formal platform to evaluate progress, resolve challenges, and ensure safety and contract compliance. 
3. Site Reports. This involves documentation of communications done on sites and reports of meetings done on memos or updated regarding construction sites. 
4. Opinions Surveys 
5. Trade unions 
6. Site forums 
7. Training Programmes and workshop
