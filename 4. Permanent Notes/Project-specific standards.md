# **Project-specific standards**

**Tags**: #p-notes #construction-mangement #construction-standard 
**Date Created: 2026-01-08:16:24**
____
Project specific standards are standards defined by clients consultants or design teams to shoot the unique need of a project. They include material specifications, workmanship requirements, performance benchmarks. Most of these standards are specifications uses guidelines from construction specification Institute.
