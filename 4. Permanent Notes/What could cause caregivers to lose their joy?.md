# **What could cause caregivers to lose their joy?**

**Tags**: #p-notes #joy #care-giving 
**Date Created: 2026-01-23:20:52**
____
Caregiver are usually busy taking care of their loved one most at times at the expense of their personal lives. Caregiving is demanding as it requires the time, energy and strength of the caregiver. Hence, the joy a caregiver has can easily be dampened.  Some cases maybe that
