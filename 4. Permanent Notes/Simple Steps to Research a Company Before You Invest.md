# **Simple Steps to Research a Company Before You Invest**

**Tags**: #p-notes #investing 
**Date Created: 2026-02-05:22:26**
____

### **1. Understand What the Company Does**
Take time to understand what the company does by asking questions like
- What problem does this company solve?
- How does it make money?
- Do people need or want what it sells?
