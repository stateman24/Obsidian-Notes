# **Simple Steps to Research a Company Before You Invest**

**Tags**: #p-notes #investing 
**Date Created: 2026-02-05:22:26**
____

### **1. Understand What the Company Does**
Take time to understand what the company does by asking questions like
- What problem does this company solve?
- How does it make money?
- Do people need or want what it sells?
### **2. Look at the Company’s Financial Health**
Numbers are key indicators of a companies financial health so it is important look at the company’s financial reports, statements and statistics. Using platforms like Tradeview or going to the companies website. Start by checking the company’s revenue, profits, and debt. Revenue shows how much money the company brings in. Profit shows what remains after expenses. Debt shows how much the company owes.
