# **Movement Regulations on Construction**

**Tags**: #p-notes #health-and-safety #construction-mangement 
**Date Created: 2026-01-08:15:35**
____
[[Health and Safety on the Construction site]] also involve some other regulations like movement regulations. Movement regulation in construction sites is important because construction sites are highly dynamic environments and they are usually characterized by constant flux in and out of personnel on site and wide spectrum of individuals such as subcontractors and engineers. For optimum and effective construction output, movement regulation in construction sites must be enforced and this involves systematic regulation of site access.
