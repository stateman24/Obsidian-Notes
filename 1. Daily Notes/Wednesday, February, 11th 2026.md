**Theme**: Today’s text highlights the importance of Christian maturity. Mature Christian usually show qualities such as wisdom, courage, generosity, and empathy and this allow them to make decisions that are spiritually acceptable albeit different from another mature Christian decision on the same matter which can be also spiritually acceptable.
